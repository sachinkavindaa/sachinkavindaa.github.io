Presented a seminar on the application of artificial intelligence, feature engineering, and ensemble machine learning methods to investigate the relationship between the rumen microbiome and feed efficiency in dairy cows.

The presentation focused on:
- Rumen microbiome analysis
- Machine learning approaches for biological data
- Feature engineering techniques
- Ensemble learning models
- Prediction of feed efficiency traits in dairy cattle
- Microbiome-driven precision livestock research

This work highlights how artificial intelligence can improve our understanding of microbial contributions to animal performance and feed utilization.
