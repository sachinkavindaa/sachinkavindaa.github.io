Worked as a Graduate Research Assistant in Animal Science at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, focusing on microbiome, metagenomics, and bioinformatics research.

Responsibilities and experience included:

* Processing microbiome and metagenomic sequencing data using High-Performance Computing (HPC) clusters
* Performing genome assembly, binning, taxonomic annotation, and functional analysis of metagenomic datasets
* Developing and running bioinformatics pipelines for metagenomics and microbial community analysis
* Conducting 16S rRNA amplicon analysis using DADA2 and downstream microbiome analysis workflows
* Using bioinformatics tools including MetaBAT2, GTDB-Tk, DRAM, Kraken2, Bowtie2, CheckM2, and MEGAHIT more. 
* Writing SLURM batch scripts for large-scale computational analysis and workflow automation
* Performing wet lab techniques including DNA extraction, PCR amplification, library preparation, and sequencing sample preparation
* Assisting with microbial culturing, laboratory sample handling, and molecular biology experiments
* Supporting collaborative microbiome and animal health research projects
