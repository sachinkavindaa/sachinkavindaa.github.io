Exploratory Data Analysis, also known as EDA, is one of the most important steps in any data science project. Before building machine learning models or making conclusions, we need to understand the dataset clearly.

In this article, I explain the basic idea of EDA, why it is important, and how beginners can use it to find patterns, missing values, outliers, and useful insights from data.

## Topics Covered

* What is Exploratory Data Analysis?
* Why EDA is important
* Understanding dataset structure
* Handling missing values
* Detecting outliers
* Visualizing data
* Finding patterns and relationships
* Preparing data for machine learning

EDA helps us ask better questions, understand the story behind the data, and make better decisions before applying advanced models.
